Is this really FREE? No hidden charges?

100% FREE for job seekers! We are a work-matching service. Companies pay us to find qualified Ghanaians. You pay absolutely nothing - no application fees, no placement fees, no monthly fees.

How long until I get my first paycheck?

Most workers receive their first payment within 7-10 days of starting work. Companies pay weekly via Mobile Money or bank transfer. Some positions offer daily payments for completed tasks!

I have no experience. Can I still apply?

YES! Many positions require no previous experience. We provide complete training for all roles. If you can use a smartphone or computer and understand basic English, you can qualify for several positions.

What equipment do I need?

Most positions require: 1) A smartphone OR computer, 2) Stable internet connection (even mobile data works), 3) A quiet place to work. Some companies may provide equipment for top performers.
