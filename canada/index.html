Frequently Asked Questions

Information about our trusted job-matching platform

Do you charge any fees to use your platform?

No, our platform is completely free for job seekers. We are a job-matching service that connects qualified candidates with verified employers. We do not charge any application, placement, or service fees from job seekers.

How does the employment process work?

We are a matching platform, not a direct employer. When you apply, we share qualified profiles with verified Canadian companies seeking remote workers. Employers contact suitable candidates directly to discuss specific positions, compensation, and employment terms.

What type of companies use your platform?

We work exclusively with legitimate, registered Canadian companies that follow all employment regulations. All employers are verified before they can post positions on our platform.

Are these real employment positions?

Yes, all positions represent legitimate employment opportunities. Successful candidates receive proper employment contracts that comply with Canadian law, including all legal rights and protections.
